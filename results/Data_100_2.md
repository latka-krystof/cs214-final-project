# Key Improvements

Compared to baseline routing strategies:

- 🚀 **9.6% lower mean latency vs Random × Random**
- 🚀 **21.5% lower mean latency vs Random Router**
- 🚀 **27% lower P99 latency**
- 🚀 **~9 seconds reduction in worst-case latency**

Our router significantly reduces **tail latency**, which is critical for production LLM serving systems.
